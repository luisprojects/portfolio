Working in the Netherlands

  • The Netherlands is known for its strong work-life balance, employee rights, and a robust economy.
  • Employees are entitled to a minimum of 20 vacation days per year.
  • The standard workweek is 40 hours, with a maximum of 12 hours per day and 60 hours per week.
  • Employees are entitled to paid sick leave and maternity/paternity leave.
  • The minimum wage is adjusted biannually and varies by age.
